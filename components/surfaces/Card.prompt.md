The workhorse surface for services, insights and capabilities.

```jsx
<Card interactive index="01" kicker="Interim Leadership"
      title="Executive on demand"
      footer={<Button variant="ghost" arrow>Learn more</Button>}>
  Seasoned C-suite leaders deployed in days to hold the line.
</Card>
```

Set `theme="ink"` inside dark bands, `href` to make the whole card a link, `interactive` for the hover lift + brass top-rule.
